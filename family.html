👩 My Supermom

My mom isn't just my mother,she’s my biggest cheerleader, my safe place, and the person who believes in me even when I don’t believe in myself. She has made so many sacrifices without ever asking for anything in return.

She’s the kind of person who puts my happiness before her own, who stays up late to help me, who never misses a single moment that matters to meeven when she’s exhausted.

Whenever I feel like giving up, she’s the one who reminds me how strong I am. Her words give me courage, and her love gives me strength. I know I wouldn’t be where I am today without her endless support.

I love my mom more than words can say. She’s not just part of my lifeshe’s the reason I keep moving forward. I love her so much! 💐

🐾 My Furry Baby

I found Anje just outside my house when he was only about a month old. A tiny kitten seeking shelter, probably abandoned by his mother and unwilling to leave. Since then, he has become an inseparable part of my family and my best friend. His playful spirit and comforting presence brighten my days, and I couldn’t imagine life without my sweet little buddy, Anje Aboy Christopher. 🐱💕🐾
