🎉 My Favorite Hobbies 🎉

📚 Reading

I love diving into books, especially fantasy and adventure genres. Reading helps me relax and sparks my imagination!

🎮 Gaming

Whenever I find some free time, I love immersing myself in video games because they not only help me unwind my problems.

My current favorites include Wuthering Wave, Honkai IStar Rail, and Genshin Impact, along with the timeless classic GTA San Andreas each offering a unique world to explore and challenge.

🎬 Watching Movies & Drama

Watching movies and dramas is one of my favorite pastimes, especially when I can get lost in thrilling horror stories or emotionally rich Thai dramas that keep me hooked episode after episode. TEERAK!!! .

😴 Sleeping

Sleep is incredibly important to me, and I truly believe there’s nothing better than curling up for a peaceful rest that recharges both my body and mind for the days ahead.

🍽️ Eating

For me, eating is more than just for surviving, it’s a joyful ritual that helps relieve stress, fills my free time with delight, and turns every meal into a little celebration of flavors and comfort.
